About us

Who are We

F.R.I.T.H stands for Force for Rapid Intervention and Tactical Help, founded by Mani Sankar. We are an elite virtual organization committed to the protection of innocent lives from threats such as crime, harrasment, and violence. Founded in silence, we work in shadows. Not for recognition, but for peace. Our teams are trained to act swiftly, think smartly and, protect fiercely.

Founded with the vision of creating a safer, kinder, and more protected world. F.R.I.T.H is more than just a tactical unit. It's a sanctuary of hope.

A digital sanctuary for those who need heroes in the shadows. We are the Guardians of Hope, The Listeners of the unheard, and The Protectors of Peace. Every member of F.R.I.T.H is driven by a fire to protect and a heart that understands pain.

Because when the world turns its back, F.R.I.T.H steps forward.

We're not here for fame or fortune. We're here for the quiet cries, the unseen battles, the overlooked lives.

Prerequisites

Please review the following requirements before proceeding:

  • Age constraints: Your age can range between 21 and 55 at the time of application
  • Educational Qualification: A Diploma or Degree in any field is mandatory.
  • Clean Background: Applicants should not have any criminal records or history of legal offenses.
  • Mental and Physical Readiness: You must be mentally stable and physically fit to handle real-world responsibilities and operations.
  • Good Communication Skills: Strong verbal and written communication skills are required for effective coordination.
  • Confidentiality Agreement: You must agree to keep sensitive information private and secure
  • Protective Mindset: F.R.I.T.H. agents must have a strong sense of responsibility and a mindset dedicated to protecting others.
  • Willingness to Serve: A genuine willingness to serve the community without personal bias or agenda is essential.
  • Verification & Training Compliance: You must be willing to go through necessary background check and complete mandatory training session.

By checking this, you agree that you fulfill the above criteria and wish to book an appointment with our Hiring recruiters.
